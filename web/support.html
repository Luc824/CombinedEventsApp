Common Questions

How do I calculate points?

Enter your performance results for each event in the app. The calculator will automatically compute your points using the official World Athletics scoring tables.

Can I save my results?

Currently, results are stored locally on your device. Make sure to keep the app installed to preserve your data.

Is the app free?

Yes! The app is free to use. If you'd like to support development, you can make a tip through the in-app donation feature.
