Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Combined Events Calculator app ("we", "our", or "app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The app operates entirely on your device. All calculation results and data remain stored locally on your device and are never sent to our servers or any third parties.

In-App Purchases

If you choose to make an in-app purchase (donation), the transaction is processed by Apple through their App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to these transactions.

Third-Party Services

The app uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their privacy policy. No personally identifiable information is shared.

Data Storage

All app data, including calculation results, is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
